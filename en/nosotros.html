About Us


Our History

In PROFESIONALES EN CONVENCIONES we conformed a team of professionals committed with the satisfaction of our clients; supported with the experience of 36 years in the corporate touristic industry.

Nowadays, Viajes Cuernavaca and Profesionales en Convenciones integrate Cuernavaca Group, being one of the pioneers and leader companies on the development of MICE located at WTC in Mexico City, counting with 1,200m2 and with more than 200 employees specialized in offering corporate services.

Supported by a strong and current infrastructure, strategic partners and reliable funds with the aim to provide the appropriate logistics for your event. With a huge background that give us the support to cover national and international market.

On the search of the continuous improvement and a global representation, Viajes Cuernavaca has joined the Lufthansa City Center (LCC) network in March 2015; with this step we have connected the high-level service of our privately owned company with the advantages of an internationally operating travel agency organization.




Achieve the total satisfaction of our clients, by organizing all kinds of events, supported by our team of highly qualified professionals, drawing on our experience.
To be the organizer company of events with more recognition in the country, through an excellent service and attention to our customers.
  • The service quality is our priority.
  • Customers are always, the most important.
  • Cordiality is the basis of good business.
  • Professionalism drives our growth.
